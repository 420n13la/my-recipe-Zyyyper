omelet

served with bread and butter. 1 Omelet produced

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 15% cream
  • fresh basil

Steps

  1. beat eggs
  2. add cream to eggs
  3. pour eggs in a pan on medium heat
  4. reduce heat to low and cover
  5. remove when you think its cooked enough for you
